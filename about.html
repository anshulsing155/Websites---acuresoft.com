Our Story

Founded in 2015, Expert HP Printer Setup Services was born from a simple observation: too many people were struggling with HP printer installations and getting frustrated with complex setup processes.

Our founder, a former HP technician with over 15 years of experience, recognized the need for specialized, USA-based support that could provide quick, reliable solutions for HP printer users across the country.

Today, we've helped over 10,000 customers get their HP printers working perfectly, from home users to large businesses, with our combination of free tools and expert support services.
